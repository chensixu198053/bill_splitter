Two separate files keep your setup and records independent. Replace the downloaded files in your project folder (or GitHub) to persist changes.

Members & Families

Stores your member list and family groups. Download after making changes, then replace the file in your project.

Stores all confirmed gathering records. Download after confirming a summary, then replace the file in your project.
